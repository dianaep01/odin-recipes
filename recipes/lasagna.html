Lasagna Recipe

This is the easiest recipe you will ever make!

Classic homemade lasagna, made the easiest way of all. 20 min prep time. That is it. And the end result?

Well, it is perfection.

Ingredients

  • grounded meat
  • 2tbs oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 box chopped tomato
  • 2tbs tomato pur√©
  • 1tbs basil
  • 0.5ts pepper
  • 0.5ts salt

How to:

  1. Fry meat and cut all veggies
  2. add all the remaining ingredients to the pan
  3. let it simmer until the sauce thickens
  4. add basil for a herbal taste
  5. Stack the meat sauce and pasta layers
  6. put in oven for 35 minutes
