Muffins Recipe

Delicious and fluffy muffins

Celebrate your birthday with some simple treats that will make everybody happy

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2dl sugar
  • 125g butter
  • 1dl milk
  • 3.5dl flour
  • 1.5ts baking powder

How to:

  1. Heat oven on 200 degrees Celsius
  2. Mix egg and sugar, and add the dry ingredients
  3. Put the mix in muffin f and let them cook for 20 minutes
  4. Decorate as you wish and enjoy!
